# 📘 Lab 03 – Date & Time Functions

## 🎯 Objective

Practice Microsoft Excel Date & Time Functions by solving real-world business scenarios using an employee dataset.

This lab focuses on calculating employee age, work experience, project timelines, working days, invoice due dates, and other common business calculations.
